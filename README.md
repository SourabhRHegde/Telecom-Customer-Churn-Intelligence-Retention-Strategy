# Telecom Customer Churn Intelligence & Retention Strategy

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior in a telecom business using Excel, MySQL, and Power BI. The objective was to identify high-risk customer segments, uncover churn drivers, and develop strategic retention recommendations through end-to-end business analytics.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow including:
- Business Understanding
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL Business Querying
- KPI Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Power BI Dashboarding
- Executive-Level Strategic Recommendations

---

## Business Problem

Telecom companies face significant revenue loss due to customer churn. This project analyzes customer behavior patterns to answer key business questions such as:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Which services influence customer retention?
- Which customer segments contribute highest risk?
- How can the company improve customer retention?

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Excel — Data Cleaning & Exploratory Analysis
- MySQL — Business Querying & Segmentation
- Power BI — Dashboard Development & Visualization

---

## Key Business Insights

- Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rate.
- Fiber optic users demonstrated elevated churn behavior.
- New customers were identified as the most vulnerable segment.
- Electronic check users showed higher churn probability.
- Customers without support/security services had significantly higher churn rates.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- Promote long-term contract adoption through targeted offers.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Bundle support and security services with internet plans.
- Encourage automated payment methods through incentives.
- Launch targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer groups.
